ABOUT EPIC

MISSION & VISION

EPIC cricket academy is a child academy formed after Kulathoor cricket academy (KRCA) and is run by a group of young cricket enthusiast in Trivandrum. One of our key member, TR Sudheesh former Kerala Renji trophy player (1998-1999) is the head coach of our academy.
The vision and mission, to provide a platform for all young aspiring cricketers in India, a place they can call home and where every opportunity will be provided to grow and most importantly have fun.
Our other coaches work closely with T R Sudheesh, so as to provide the finest coaching programs for school, club, amateur and professional cricketers of our academy.
Come and join our team of Highly skilled, fully qualified coaches who deliver an unparalleled cricketing experience in world class facilities.
